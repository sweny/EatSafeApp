Eat Safe

An app that helps you stay healthy

Don't let your food take you by surprise. Read the label!

According to FDA (Food and Drug Administration), a key to choosing healthful food is to use the nutrition facts on the food label.

Why Eat Safe?

People tend to overlook personal health constraints while consuming food. Results show that 65% of adult Americans are obese and estimated annual medical cost of obesity in the U.S. was $147 billion. As per American Society for Clinical Nutrition, the proportion of food consumed from restaurants and fast food outlets increased nearly by 300%. These changes in food intake explain the rise in blood pressure, insulin level and cholesterol concentration and underscore the importance of healthy eating habits.

How we help you stay healthy?

Our app helps you make quick, informed food choices while buying and consuming food. The app tracks and analyzes the nutriotional, caloric and allergic information of food products scanned in real time and provide suggestions based on the user's health profile that contribute to healthy lifelong eating habits.
